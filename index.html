Countess Wear Scarecrow Trail

15th-22nd February 2021

ABOUT

Building a scarecrow together to help spread some cheer would be a great family activity and hopefully a welcome distraction from lockdown! Let us know if you'd like to add a scarecrow to the trail, we have 3 so far and hopefully will have more to add to a map for everyone.

They don't have to be fancy, any old bits and bobs you have around to put one together will do! We're hoping to spread a few smiles and some cheer by giving everyone something different to look out for on walks or exercise outings

We'll also be raising money for Devon hospicecare
